About me

My name is Félix Barreto, I am 56 years old, and I am from Venezuela. I live in a small town called Valle de la Pascua, which belongs to the state of Guárico, in the central part of the country, in the Venezuelan plains. The main economic activities in the area are agriculture and cattle ranching. Currently, my brother and I work on our farm where we raise dairy cows and grow corn. My favorite hobby when I'm relaxing at home is playing chess and watching biopic.
